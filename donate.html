Support Us

We invite you to join us in our journey of walking together towards a sustainable and prosperous future. You can support us in various ways, such as:

  • Donate: You can make a donation to support our projects and activities. You can choose to donate to a specific project or to our general fund. You can also donate in kind, such as equipment, materials, or services. You can make a donation online through our website, or offline through cheque, bank transfer, or cash.
  • Volunteer: You can volunteer with us and contribute your time, skills, and energy to our work. You can volunteer in the field, in the office, or remotely, depending on your availability and preference. You can volunteer as an individual or as a group, for a short-term or a long-term period.
  • Partner: You can partner with us and collaborate with us on our projects and activities. You can partner with us as a government agency, a private sector entity, a civil society organization, an academic institution, a media outlet, or an international organization. You can partner with us in various ways, such as co-funding, co-implementing, co-evaluating, co-promoting, or co-learning.
  • Spread the Word: You can spread the word about our work and our cause, and help us reach out to more people and stakeholders. You can follow us on our social media platforms, such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube, and like, comment, and share our posts. You can also subscribe to our newsletter and blog, and read and share our stories and updates. You can also write or talk about us in your own platforms, such as blogs, podcasts, magazines, or newspapers.

About Us

Gram Saarthi, an NGO active in Central India's tribal areas, aligns its mission with Sustainable Development Goals, leveraging traditional knowledge for innovative solutions.

Through collaborations with stakeholders and community engagement, it strives to empower tribal populations for sustainable development and improved well-being.
